# Business Urgency Detection

## Escalate immediately when
- System/service outage reported by client or team
- Payment or financial issue requiring action today
- Legal or compliance deadline within 24 hours
- Client escalation or threat to leave
- Security incident or data breach
- Approval blocking others' work (they're waiting on you)
- Meeting or call starting soon that you might miss

## Do NOT escalate
- Routine status updates ("project is on track")
- FYI messages about non-urgent business matters
- Marketing or promotional messages from vendors
- Newsletter or subscription content
- Invitations to events more than 48 hours away

## Priority levels
- CRITICAL: outage, security, legal (immediate action)
- HIGH: client escalation, blocking approval (within hours)
- MEDIUM: deadline approaching, opportunity timing (within a day)
